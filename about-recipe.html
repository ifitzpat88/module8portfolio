A Family Tradition

I picked up this Beef Chili Mac & Cheese recipe from my mom, who often made it for us when we were kids. It's simple, filling, and has been a favorite in our family for years.

Why This Recipe is Special

  • It combines the hearty flavors of chili with the comfort of mac and cheese
  • Perfect for busy weeknights or large family gatherings
  • Easily customizable to suit different tastes and dietary needs

This recipe has evolved over the years, with each family member adding their own twist. The addition of bell peppers was my contribution, adding a fresh crunch and extra nutrition to this beloved dish.
